Programs Designed to Inspire, Train,
and Transform Young Minds

Straight Path Athletics offers a variety of structured sports and character development programs that build physical strength, leadership, and spiritual grounding. Each program is rooted in our mission to empower youth through faith, mentorship, and sportsmanship.

Explore Programs

Fall Camp 2025

September – November | Ages 8–14

A foundational football & basketball program focused on conditioning, teamwork, and skill-building. Balanced with Islamic character lessons and mentorship.

Winter Indoor League

January – February | Ages 10–16

Indoor 3v3 and 5v5 tournaments with weekly training. Focused on sharpening performance while reinforcing Islamic values and sportsmanship.

Spring Training Series

March – May | Ages 7–12

Focused on agility, endurance, and teamwork through structured drills and faith-based leadership sessions. Great for new and returning athletes.
